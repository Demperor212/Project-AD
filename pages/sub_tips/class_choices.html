How to choose a class?

Check your desired university program's requirement, choose what they want you to choose.

Dash 1 for math and english is the only choice for you if you want to go to a university.

Dash 2 for social studies is acceptable, as most universities does not care about social studies.

Take bio or physics plus chemistry, depending on what path you want to take in university.

Take electives/options ONLY if you feel like time is at your disposal and you are not satisfied with your spare.

If you are taking IB, I highly recommend not take electives during your busy times (that is semester 3 and 4, when you are crying over your ia or IB exam)

And don't take IB computer science and one of the three other sciences like bio/physics/chem!!!!!!! If you take IB com sci you still need to take two other proper sciences for a better chance for uni application!!!!!!
