Should I do math contest?

It is recommended that you do math contest if you can score a distinction (top 25%), or with a bit of effort you can score a distinction.

At least U waterloo will absolutely love you if you do CSMC/ Euclid and score a decent score.

If you somehow make it to the honor roll (top 5%), there will usually be huge scholarship for you if you go to U waterloo.

Honor roll is hard, but distinction is usually easy: If you can have a good school grade you can probably get distinction.

If you want to prep for math contests, the sooner, the better, as I prepped a bit too late (only started to prep around 5 months before contest) and now suffering from brain implosions.
